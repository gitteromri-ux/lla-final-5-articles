What makes the promise concrete is that no student is handed generic advice. Inside the flagship Longevity Blueprint, every US student receives an Abbott Lingo continuous glucose monitor — shipped free to their home before the CGM module in Lesson 5 — and learns to read their own glucose, sleep, and recovery data as a live feedback loop. Gibson Clark will lead those live cohorts alongside a named faculty that includes longevity researcher Natalie Blackbourne, University of California, Santa Barbara senior lecturer Amy Jamieson, integrative nutrition coach Courtney Donofrio, and behavioral specialist Jordan Lattimore — practitioners who know every student by name.

"We are focused on impact and results, and Julie's results speak for themselves," said eTeacher CEO Harel Tayeb. "She is living proof that world-class longevity is about consistent, teachable habits — not spending millions — and that is exactly the school we are building." Gibson Clark begins teaching with the current enrollment cohort.
